WIDER Face and Pedestrian Challenge 2018

Workshop in conjunction with ECCV 2018, Munich, Germany
September 8, 2018

Challenge Tracks

We organize a new challenge in conjunction with ECCV 2018. The challenge centers around the problem of precise localization of human faces and bodies, and accurate association of identities. It comprises of three tracks. The first track, WIDER Face, aims at soliciting new approaches to advance the state-of- the-art in face detection. The second track, WIDER Pedestrian, has the goal of gathering effective and efficient approaches to address the problem of pedestrian detection in unconstrained environments. The third track, WIDER Person Search, presents an exciting challenge of matching persons across 192 movies. More details will be announced in May, stay tuned.
